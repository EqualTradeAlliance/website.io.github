About us

We are an international NGO that promotes fair and sustainable trade, empowering small producers, educating consumers and collaborating with companies to build a more equitable and responsible economic system.

Mission

Promote fair trade globally, ensuring decent working conditions, fair prices and sustainable practices. We work to empower small producers, raise consumer awareness and collaborate with companies to build a more equitable and responsible trading system.

Vision

To create a world where trade is synonymous with fairness, sustainability and equitable opportunities for all. We aspire to ensure that every product traded has a positive impact on the producing communities and the environment.

Our impact on fair trade

EqualTrade Alliance has improved access to international markets for small producers, guaranteeing fair prices and better working conditions. Through training and certification programs, we help strengthen their skills and improve the quality of their products.

Educational Resources

At EqualTrade Alliance, we believe that education is key to fairer and more sustainable trade. That's why we offer a variety of educational resources, courses, workshops and mentorships so that producers, businesses and consumers can learn, develop new skills and make informed decisions.

  • Fair Trade Library

    Access a collection of articles, research and guides on fair trade, sustainability and responsible consumption.

  • Courses and Workshops

    We offer training programs for producers, businesses and consumers who want to learn more about fair trade and sustainable practices.

    • For Producers: Sustainable production techniques, certifications and market access.
    • For Companies: Integration of fair trade in the supply chain and social responsibility.
    • For Consumers: How to make responsible purchasing decisions and support fair trade.

  • Online chats and events

    Participate in our online chats and events with fair trade experts, ethical companies and producers.

  • Mentorship Program

    Connect with fair trade experts and receive personalized advice to implement sustainable practices in your business or community.

  • Community and Learning Network

    Join forums and discussion groups where producers, companies and consumers share knowledge, experiences and advice on fair trade.

Join the cause

Fair trade is not just a practice, it is a global movement that needs everyone's participation. EqualTrade Alliance offers a variety of ways in which companies, producers and consumers can get involved to drive a fairer and more sustainable trading system.

For Companies: Trade with Purpose

Companies play a key role in the transformation of the market towards a more equitable model. If you are a company interested in integrating fair trade practices, we offer:

  • Access to fair trade products: Incorporate certified products in your supply chain.
  • Certification and standards compliance: We help you obtain fair trade and sustainability certifications.
  • Connecting with ethical producers: Work directly with communities that produce fairly and responsibly.
  • Strategic alliances: Collaborate with other companies committed to fair trade.
  • Corporate social responsibility: Improve your corporate image by aligning yourself with values of equity and sustainability.

For Producers: Fair and Sustainable Opportunities

If you are a small or medium scale producer looking for better market opportunities under fair conditions, EqualTrade Alliance provides you with tools and support to strengthen your business:

  • Training and consulting: You will learn about sustainable practices, marketing and export strategies.
  • Fair trade certification: We guide you through the certification process so that your products are recognized in international markets.
  • Access to global markets: We connect producers with buyers committed to equity.
  • Better commercial conditions: Receive fair prices for your work, without abusive intermediaries.
  • Community development: We promote economic and social growth in the producing communities.

For Consumers: Be Part of the Change

As a consumer or volunteer, you have the power to influence the way the world trades. Your support contributes directly to improving the lives of producers and fostering a fairer system.

  • Buying with impact: Choose fair trade products and support small producers
  • Volunteer: Join our programs and events to help expand our cause.
  • Be an ambassador: Share information, spread the word about our mission and raise awareness in your community.
  • Donate and support projects: Contribute resources to help producers and finance sustainable initiatives.

Together we can build a future where trade benefits everyone, not just a few. Choose how you want to get involved and join the EqualTrade Alliance today.

Commitment to Fair Trade

At EqualTrade Alliance, we are dedicated to ensuring that small-scale producers receive fair compensation for their work, allowing them to improve their quality of life and invest in their communities. We advocate for ethical trade practices that eliminate worker exploitation, promote gender equality, and protect human rights.

By choosing us, you support a global movement that prioritizes people over profits and builds a more equitable economic system.

Global and Local Impact

Our work extends across Latin America, Africa, and Asia, where we empower local producers with the resources and training needed to compete in international markets. Through fair trade practices, we help farmers and small businesses reach conscious consumers in Europe, North America, and Asia.

Our impact goes beyond fair trade, we help local economies, preserve cultural traditions and create sustainable lifestyles.

Certification and Transparency

We believe that trust is built through transparency. Our certification process ensures that products meet rigorous ethical, social, and environmental standards. We work closely with producers to improve their practices, while also providing businesses and consumers with the assurance that their purchases contribute to positive change.

With knowledge and communication, we ensure that every step of the supply chain is fair and has integrity.

Education and Awareness

Creating meaningful change starts with informed decisions. We provide workshops, training programs, and awareness campaigns to educate consumers, businesses, and students on the impact of their purchases. By advocating for ethical consumerism and corporate responsibility, we foster a shift toward a more sustainable and socially responsible market.

Through online resources, public events, and advocacy initiatives, we empower individuals and organizations to create meaningful change.

Collaborative Network

EqualTrade Alliance unites producers, ethical businesses, nonprofits, and activists to build a strong network dedicated to transforming global trade. Through strategic partnerships, we enhance supply chains, create new market opportunities for small-scale producers, and drive innovation in sustainable business practices.

Collaboration is at the core of our mission, ensuring that fair trade creates lasting benefits for all.

Environmental Commitment

Sustainability is a core pillar of our work. We promote eco-friendly production methods that minimize environmental impact, conserve natural resources, and protect biodiversity. From advocating for organic farming and reducing carbon footprints to encouraging sustainable packaging solutions, we help businesses and producers adopt responsible practices.

Our commitment to the planet goes hand in hand with our mission to support people, ensuring that trade benefits both communities and the environment for generations to come.
